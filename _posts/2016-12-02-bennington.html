DESCRIPTION

Bennington Review needed a website to both display the work in their literary review and sell the print journal. The main design goal of the site was text-based, giving the work the main stage. We chose Squarespace for its eCommerce capabilities and the clean, font-centric look.

PROJECT DETAILS

  • TASK: Web Design, eCommerce, Branding, Graphic Design
