PRAIRIE LIGHTS BOOKSTORE TOP 10 NOVEMBER 2014

1) Lila - Marilynne Robinson
2) Some Luck - Jane Smiley
3) Calenday - Lauren Haldeman
4) My Prairie Cookbook - Melissa Gilbert
5) Loitering - Charles D’Ambrosio
6) Not That Kind of Girl - Lena Dunham
7) Ambitions - Joseph Dobrian
Occupants - Henry Rollins
8) Yes Please - Amy Poehler
Wahls Protocol - Terry Wahls
9) Being Mortal - Atul Gawande
10) Hild - Nicola Griffith

Aww, look at Calenday. Just hanging out up there at #3, with... wait WHAT?

Well, this just freaking blows my mind. Makes me basically the happiest.  And the happy part of my mind now thinks I could just stop doing everything, because it doesn't get better than this. But then the other, meaner part of my mind says "You won't do anything better than this. Don't even try." Which is like the same voice? Saying the same thing? Sometimes I don't understand my mind, at all.

Anyhow, thanks to all my friends who came to the reading and bought the book, because look! Look!
