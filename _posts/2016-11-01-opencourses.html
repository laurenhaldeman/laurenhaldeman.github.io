DESCRIPTION

Tasked with developing and designing a website to house a Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) for the University of Iowa, I built this site on Drupal 7, resulting in the launch of the UI’s first MOOC, Every Atom: Walt Whitman’s Song of Myself, which enrolled over 2,100 participants from over 25 countries. I was in charge of building the website, designing the course user experience, organizing the content and curriculum online and responding technically to individual users and the growing needs of the project.
