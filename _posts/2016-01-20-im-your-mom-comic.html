Working on another comic for the poem "I'm Your Mom" in Calenday. Trying to create a world where a mom ties her baby on her back and goes out hunting with a bow and arrow -- this was a kind of daydream that I leaned on when E was young. I was trying to feel strong, in the midst of what seemed like endless weakness. Anyhow, here's some drawkings about it! I'm at the painting stage now.
