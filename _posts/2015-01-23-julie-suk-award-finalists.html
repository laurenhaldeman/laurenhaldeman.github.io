A list of all the finalists and where to get them!

Calenday was just nominated for the Julie Suk Award, an award given by Jacar Press for the best book of poetry published in 2014, and that made so happy! Maybe this means the book isn't a complete disaster, right? (As Kelli Russell Agodon hilariously put it.) It was nominated along with all these other amazing finalists -- and their collections instantly caught my eye. I suddenly wanted all of those books. I imagined a giant package of them arriving at my gate, carried by a dog sled and wrapped in brown-paper; I pictured myself in wool pajamas, petting the huskies and giving the driver a magical tincture in a bronze flask, as payment for the long trek.

But it didn't seem like there was one button I could push to make all that happen. There wasn't, like, an "All These Books By Dog Sled" button. So in lieu of that, I am compiling the list here, of where to get them, for me, for you -- for everyone!
