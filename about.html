OH, HELLO THERE

I’m Lauren Haldeman.

I am the author of the graphic novel TEAM PHOTOGRAPH (Sarabande Books, 2022), and the poetry collections
INSTEAD OF DYING (winner of the Colorado Prize for Poetry)
CALENDAY (Rescue Press, 2014)
and THE ECCENTRICITY IS ZERO (Digraph Press, 2014).

I've received the Iowa Artist Fellowship, the Sustainable Arts Foundation Award, the Colorado Prize for Poetry & the fellowships from the Iowa Writers’ Workshop.

My work has appeared in Poetry, Tin House, Colorado Review, Fence, The Iowa Review and The Rumpus.

I work as a web developer, web designer and editor during the daytime. Also: I’m a mom and make paintings.
